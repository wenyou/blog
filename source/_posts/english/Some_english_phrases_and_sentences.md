* many good things happening  好事连连 / 诸多美事正在发生 / 喜事不断
> 完整句式相当于：Many good things are happening.
>> Many good things happening lately. 最近好事连连。
>> Keep calm, many good things are happening. 别着急，很多好事正在路上。
>> Life is beautiful, with many good things happening around us. 生活很美好，身边总有诸多好事发生。
>> Pool ran into the CEOs. Asked how meetings were going. Elon Musk said it was going “wonderful” and “many good things” happening. Tim Cook gave peace sign into thumbs up. Jensen Haung said “meetings went well” and Xi and Trump are “incredible.”

* Home Alone
> 小鬼当家：一部1990年的美国喜剧电影，讲述了一个小男孩在家中独自面对两个笨贼的故事。
>> VP Vance says he feels a little like Macaulay Culkin in "Home Alone" with Trump out of country. The @VP doesn't travel with president outside US. 副总统万斯说，特朗普出国期间，他感觉自己有点像电影《小鬼当家》里的麦考利·卡尔金。副总统不会随总统出访美国境外。
